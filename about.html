About ENSTA

The Higher National School of Advanced Technologies is an Algerian engineering university dedicated to excelence in education , research, and innovation in the fields of advanced and emerging technologies.Located in Algiers.

Our Mission

ENSTA's mission is to train a highly qualified engineers and researchers in computer science, artificial intelligence , Cybersecurity and other advanced fields

Our Vision

To become a regional and international leader in technological education and applied research driving Algeria toward a digital economy.

Our Values

  • Excellence: Commitment to the highest academic and professional standards.
  • Innovation: Encouraging creativity and entrepreneurship in technological fields.
  • Integrity: Promoting ethical conduct and responsibility in education and research.
  • Collaboration: Building strong partnerships with universities, industries, and research centers.
