Overview

The Higher National School of Advanced Technologies is a national engineering institute dedicated to excellence in computer science and emerging technologies. It offers a rigorous and progressive program designed to train future engineers, researchers, and technology innovators.

First Cycle : Mathematics And Computer Science (MI)

Duration: 2 years

Focus: Foundational scientific and technical education in:

  • Advanced Mathematics
  • Algorithms and Programming (C, Java)
  • Computer Architecture and Operating Systems
  • Logic Circuits, Assembly, and Data Structures
  • A solid base in Physics: Mechanics, Fundamental Electronics

Goal: Build a strong base in both mathematical reasoning and computer science fundamentals.

Second Cycle : Engineering Specialization

Duration: 3 years

Tracks:

  • Artificial Intelligence (AI) : Machine Learning, Data Science, and Intelligent Systems
  • Cybersecurity : Network Security and Digital Protection

Goal: Develop deep technical expertise and practical engineering experience through projects and internships.
